Alovera

Aloe vera is a succulent plant renowned for its numerous medicinal and cosmetic benefits. It features thick, fleshy leaves that are green to grey-green, often with serrated edges adorned with small white teeth.
Originating from the Arabian Peninsula, this resilient plant now thrives in tropical, semi-tropical, and arid climates worldwide. Aloe vera is cherished for its gel, which is widely used to soothe burns, sunburns, and cuts, thanks to its healing properties.
Additionally, it is a popular ingredient in skin care and hair care products due to its moisturizing and hydrating qualities.
Aloe vera is also easy to cultivate, requiring minimal watering and thriving in bright, indirect sunlight, making it a favored houseplant for both its practicality and its air-purifying abilities.
